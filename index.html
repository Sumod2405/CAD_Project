About Landmark

Landmark excels in providing top-tier engineering and survey drafting solutions, meticulously tailored to meet the unique demands of each project. We combine extensive expertise with a flexible approach to deliver precise, innovative results that are efficient and cost-effective.

Since inception, we have transformed complex CAD challenges into tangible business outcomes using state-of-the-art technology and a dedicated team of professionals.

Vision

To be a global partner in the AEC software industry — shaping the future, sharing knowledge and setting benchmarks for innovative CAD services.

Mission

Provide professional CAD and drafting solutions to AEC and software companies, emphasizing quality, professionalism and continuous learning.

Services

Comprehensive CAD solutions tailored to engineering and surveying needs

Drafting — Civil Engineering

Survey Mapping & Land Surveying

Swift and accurate land surveying and mapping services, compliant with global standards and tailored for field-to-office workflows.

Digital Marketing for AEC Firms

Branding & marketing strategies to increase visibility and reputation for engineering and construction businesses.

Quick Response & 24/7 Support

Support across time zones to ensure project continuity and timely responses for urgent deliverables.

Training & Continuous Learning

Flexible Engagement Models

Outsourcing models to fit budgets and scale — from staff augmentation to project-based delivery.

Tech Enabled Interior Design

Our design thesis allows us to create an atmosphere of collaboration, efficiency, cultural prosperity and sustainability. Our design technology enables powerful virtual walkthrough experiences, allowing clients to make changes before execution.

Build and Contracting

Technology has revolutionised how we build spaces. Our tech-powered process for empanelment, induction, upskilling and day-to-day contracting management helps create a curated network of skilled partners capable of delivering large-scale projects.

Project Management

Ensuring on-time delivery of projects calls for robust process controls. Our project management technology automates complex workflows and empowers managers to handle cost control, scheduling, procurement and risk assessment effectively.
